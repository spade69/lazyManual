Balight APP User Guide

Thank you for choosing Balight. This Balight APP User Guide will provide detailed descriptions to your using Balight in its full features. Please enjoy the Beyond Awesome experience.

Power on your Balight

  • Step 1 – Check that the battery is charged full and in good condition. The blue LEDs will indicate the battery level.
  • Step 2 – Install the battery on your Balight.
  • Step 3 – Press and hold the Power Button until the LED turns on. The LED should light up in 5 seconds. If the LED is not on, contact support at support@balight.com.

Connect your Balight to your Balight App

  • Step 1 – Install the Balight App onto your smartphone. For iOS users, you can download it by searching Balight in the App Store.

    For Android users, you can download it by searching Balight in the Google Play Store.

  • Step 2 – Open the Balight App. A brief introduction of the APP features will be presented to you.

  • Step 3 – Click the “Open” button to enter the APP. Please allow the APP to use your device’s Bluetooth.

    Please also allow the APP to access your location to enable the cycling features.

  • Step 4 – Make sure your Bluetooth on your smartphone is turned on. To connect the app to your Balight, click on the wheels on the bicycle diagram.

    (Clicking on the front wheel of the diagram will pair your Balight on front wheel and the same with the rear wheel)

  • Step 5 –Press and hold the power button for 5 seconds until the 4 LED strips light up.

    You will see the LEDs light up with three color bands in Red, Green, Blue or Black. On the APP, match the color combination.

  • Step 6 – Press “Next” to confirm. If colors are matched properly, a Balight ID will appear in the App.

    If the Balight ID does not appear, repeat step 5. When the Balight ID has appeared, click “Sync” to finish the pairing process.

  • Step 7 – After the pairing process is finished, you will not need to pair your Balight again (If you do not delete the device).

Play Pictures on your Balight

You have the four sources add to your collection of pictures to play on your Balight. You may download images from the cloud (it requires you to register), import from your phone, take pictures with your smartphone camera or create new pictures using the DIY function.

  • Step 1 –Select images from the collection of pictures. If you want to add to your collection of pictures, press the ADD button.

  • Step 2 –Click the “Send” button and the App will send the pictures to the Balight.

Cycling Data

  • The Balight App also features a useful way of tracking your cycling data. Select the second option of the bottom function lane. From here, you will see useful information like the remaining battery of your Balight, your current cycling speed, distance, time, and max speed. You can even record your bike route by clicking the GPS button. Keep track of all of this in real time.

Cloud (Require registration)

  • The Balight Cloud feature is where you can download additional images for your Balight. Press the ADD button on your home screen and select “Cloud.” Our Cloud Image Gallery, will be updated regularly so feel free to check in every now and then for new images.

DIY

  • DIY “Do it Yourself” is the Balight option for creating your own images for your Balight. If you like to add your own touch to things, this is where you will have the ability to design your images, either from scratch, taking a photo with your camera, or importing a photo from your photo library.

Setting Options

The third option of the button function lane is settings. You may manage your Balight features from here.

  • Photo Brightness – Slide to set your desired brightness level.

  • Play Interval – If you are playing multiple images on your Balight device, you can set up the duration of images played.

  • Device Sleep – This option allows you to set how long your Balight device will remain active after it disconnects from the app.

  • Device Picture – This option allows you to see the current playing pictures on your Balight.

  • Reverse Display – This option allows you to reverse the display of your Balight pictures.

  • Select Mode – This option allows you to select corresponding version of your Balight.

Power off your Balight device

  • To power off your Balight device, hold down the power button for about 10 seconds and your display will power off.
